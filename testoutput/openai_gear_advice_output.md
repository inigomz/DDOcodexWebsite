## 1. Overall verdict

**Structurally valid, but not fully optimized yet.**  
The planner reports **0 validation errors**, the build is using **handwraps** via **Storm’s Harness**, and the armor slot is **Firefly Garb**, which satisfies the cloth / robe / outfit requirement for a Monk-style setup.

As a level 34 Wisdom-based tactical Monk, the gearset has a solid foundation: Wisdom coverage, Stunning support, Combat Mastery, Dodge, PRR/MRR, Constitution, Resistance, and several survivability layers are present. However, it is still missing some important endgame optimization pieces, especially **Insightful Stunning**, several **artifact/set-style bonuses**, and some defensive/damage refinements.

The planner shows:

- **23 / 46 targets met**
- **23 remaining gaps**
- **7 normal augment assignments**
- **4 crafting augment assignments**
- **17 normal augment slots still open**
- **8 relevant stacking conflicts**

So: **valid and usable**, but still has meaningful optimization work remaining.

## 6. Stacking conflicts that still matter

The build has several stacking conflicts, but most are not catastrophic. They are mainly slot-efficiency issues.

### More relevant conflicts

#### Wisdom +14 duplicated
- Winning: **Wisdom +14** on Radiant Ring of Taer Valaestas
- Suppressed: **Wisdom +14** on Perfected Jidz-Tet’ka

Because Wisdom is the primary stat, this matters as an opportunity-cost issue. One of those duplicated Wisdom effects is not adding value.

However, Perfected Jidz-Tet’ka also provides other important effects, including Insightful Resistance, Insightful Sheltering, Exceptional Wisdom, and its listed set-related effects, so this is not automatically a reason to drop it.

---

#### Insightful Wisdom conflict
- Winning: **Insightful Wisdom +7** on Legendary Lamordian Top Hat
- Suppressed: **Insightful Wisdom +4** on Epic Belt of the Red Abishai

This is acceptable. The Top Hat is clearly the better Insightful Wisdom source. The belt’s Insightful Wisdom is just not contributing.

---

#### Doublestrike 16% duplicated
- Winning: **Doublestrike 16%** on Legendary Eternal Flameguard’s Lenses
- Suppressed: **Doublestrike 16%** on Legendary Breaker’s Bauble

This is a real efficiency issue. Both items are providing the same non-stacking Doublestrike value. Since Breaker’s Bauble also gives Insightful Doublestrike, Accuracy, and Armor-Piercing, it may still be worth keeping, but the duplicated base Doublestrike is wasted.

---

#### Quality Deadly +3 duplicated multiple times
- Winning: **Quality Deadly +3** on Legendary Eternal Flameguard’s Lenses
- Suppressed: **Quality Deadly +3** on Radiant Ring of Taer Valaestas
- Suppressed: **Quality Deadly +3** on Death’s Deferral

This is a notable efficiency problem because three items carry the same Quality Deadly value and only one counts. Not build-breaking, but it is a good place to look for future item swaps.

#### Deadly conflict

This is minor. You are only losing the lower Deadly source on the weapon because the goggles provide a better value.

### Mostly acceptable leftovers

#### Enhancement bonus conflict

## 7. Suggested next optimization targets

### Target 1: Find Insightful Stunning
This is the cleanest tactical upgrade. The build already has a good base tactical package, but missing **Insightful Stunning +6** leaves a large DC gap.

**Search priority:** item or augment/crafting change that adds Insightful Stunning without breaking cloth armor, handwraps, Wisdom, or existing tactical coverage.

---

### Target 2: Solve the missing artifact bonuses
The largest score penalties are from missing artifact-style bonuses:

- Artifact Armor-Piercing
- Artifact Doublestrike
- Artifact Tactical DC

If this is intended as a true final endgame set, this is the biggest structural optimization target. The current gear is valid, but the set-bonus layer is incomplete relative to the planner’s goals.

---

### Target 3: Reduce duplicate high-value stats
Look for swaps that preserve the important effects while removing wasted overlaps:

- Duplicate **Wisdom +14**
- Duplicate **Doublestrike 16%**
- Triple **Quality Deadly +3**
- Suppressed **Insightful Wisdom +4**
- Suppressed **Dodge +9%**

Do not chase perfect cleanliness at the cost of losing key tactical or defensive stats, but these are the best places to recover item efficiency.

---

### Target 4: Improve defensive stacking layers
After the tactical gaps, look for:

- Better PRR / Physical Sheltering toward **38**
- Better MRR / Magical Sheltering toward **38**
- **Quality Physical Sheltering**
- **Exceptional Healing Amplification**
- Possibly better Constitution coverage if it can reach the planner’s **+15** target cleanly

---

### Target 5: Address Enhanced Ki
The build is Monk-focused, and the planner flags **Enhanced Ki** as missing. This is not as urgent as Insightful Stunning or the artifact bonuses, but it is a good class-specific improvement target.

---

### Target 6: Use the 17 open normal augment slots intelligently
The current assigned augments cover the important basics. The remaining open slots should be used after deciding whether the item layout changes.

Do not lock in all filler augments too early if you still plan to restructure gear for Insightful Stunning or artifact bonuses.

---

## 8. Short final recommendation

**Keep the current augment and Lamordia crafting plan as the baseline.** The build is valid for a Wisdom-based cloth Monk using handwraps, and it already covers many important tactical and defensive stats.

For the next optimization pass, prioritize:

1. **Add Insightful Stunning**
2. **Fix missing artifact Tactical DC / Doublestrike / Armor-Piercing bonuses**
3. **Reduce duplicated Wisdom, Doublestrike, and Quality Deadly**
4. **Improve PRR/MRR layers and missing healing amp/sheltering layers**
5. **Find Enhanced Ki if possible**

Overall: **playable and structurally sound, but not yet a fully polished final endgame tactical Monk set.**
